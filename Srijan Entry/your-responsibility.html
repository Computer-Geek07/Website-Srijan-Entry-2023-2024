What can you do?

Don't be too hard on yourself.

Often, the personal responsibility side of climate change gets overplayed, so don't be too hard on yourself if you forget to carpool, for example. Even if you eliminated 100% of the greenhouse emissions that you use throughout your life, you would save about 1 second of emissions produced by the energy sector..

Systematic Change.

We need to pass laws that incentivize carbon-capture technologies and promote funding for new research for alternatives. If you can invest, please do, to further improve research for these technologies, as they will help in our future. Everyone will be a little unhappy with our solutions, due to their interests, but that is ok. Only when we accept that everyone cannot be satisfied, will we make real progress.

Select people who really care

We have to elect leaders who are willing to invest time, and money, in solving this climate crisis. Even if we show our current leaders, that we care, those who are in power can start making plans immediately. We have to make sure that everyone cares about the climate, and addresses the same, along with its key issues.

Remember, we're all in this together. It's not just you - it's one world.
