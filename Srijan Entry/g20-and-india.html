About G20 2023

The Group of Twenty (G20) is the primary framework for economic cooperation on a global scale. It is crucial for forming and advancing international ties and the global community. The G20 is not only symbolic; it offers a chance to promote measures that give developing and emerging economies top priority in terms of policies and efforts.

349 million people in 79 countries face acute food insecurity.

G20's Relavance to the Theme

The conference will be convened by the twenty biggest economies in the world. Guest nations are also welcomed to contribute to the talks and focus on solutions. The tenets of "One Earth, One Family, One Future" shall act as a guide for addressing future global issues collaboratively at a global scale.

The Observer Research Foundation (ORF) has labelled India to be the “Voice of the Global South”. As Indian citizens, it is our duty to strive forward not only for the global community, but also nationally and locally with the tenets of “One Earth, One Family, One Future”, and show the world that India can spearhead the change that it desperately needs.

The Emergence of G20

After its establishment in 1999 in light of the Asian Financial Crisis. the G20 Summit has been conducted annually with a rotating Presidency in charge. The G20's agenda has now been expanded to cover topics including trade, sustainable development, health, agriculture, energy, environment, climate change, and anti-corruption. The G20 initially concentrated mostly on broad macroeconomic issues.
